## $\displaystyle \delta ^{3}(\vec{r})=\delta(x)\delta (y)\delta(z)$

## $\displaystyle \int_{\Omega} \delta^{3}(\vec{r}- \vec{r}_{0}) \, \mathrm{d}v=1$

* $\displaystyle \Omega$ is the volume to integrate over
