#Biochemistry 
The process of marking a protein with ubiquitin marks it for proteolysis
## Enzymes
* E1
	* With ATP, forms thiol ester bond with ubiquitin
* E2
	* Exchanges thiol ester bond with E1
	* While connected to E3, positions ubiquitin to add to substrate/target protein
* E3
	* Recognizes [[degron tag]] on target protein
