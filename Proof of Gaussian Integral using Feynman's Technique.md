Proves the [[Gaussian Integral]] using [[Feynman's Technique]]
$$
\begin{align}
\int_{-\infty }^{\infty} e^{-x^{2}} \, \mathrm{d}x&=2\int_{0}^{\infty} e^{-x^{2}} \, \mathrm{d}x\text{ because }e^{-x^{2}}\text{ is an even function} \\
&=2I
\end{align}
$$
$$
\begin{align}
I(t)=\int_{0}^{\infty} f \, \mathrm{d}x 
\end{align}
$$
