#Physics 
## $\displaystyle \beta=\frac{1}{\tau}=\frac{1}{k_{\text{B}}T}=\frac{ \partial \sigma }{ \partial E }$
* $\displaystyle \tau$ is the [[Fundamental Temperature]]
* $\displaystyle T$ is [[temperature]]
* $\displaystyle k_{B}$ is the [[Boltzmann constant]]
