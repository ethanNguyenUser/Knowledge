$$
\ket{\Psi} =
\begin{bmatrix}
\Psi(x_{1}) \\
\Psi(x_{2}) \\
\Psi(x_{3}) \\

$$
$$
\bra{\Psi} =(\ket{\Psi} ^{\top})^{*}=
\begin{bmatrix}
\Psi(x_{1})^{*} & \Psi(x_{2})^{*} & \Psi(x_{3})^{*} & \ldots & 
\end{bmatrix}
$$
* The bra of a wave function is the complex conjugate of the transpose of the ket of the wave function
