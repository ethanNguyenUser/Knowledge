## $\displaystyle \int_{0}^{\infty} x^{2n}e^{-x^{2}/a^{2}} \, \mathrm{d}x=\sqrt{ \pi } \frac{(2n)!}{n!}\left( \frac{a}{2} \right)^{2n+1}$
