#Chemistry
Infrared light goes from 400 to 4000 $\displaystyle \mathrm{cm^{-1}}$, or 
## Zones of IR Spectroscopy
1. The Polar $\displaystyle \ce{H}$'s
2. The $\displaystyle \ce{C-H}$'s
3. The Triple Bonds
4. The Carbonyls
5. The Double Bonds
